\## 📌 Business Problem



\*\*Only 42.7% of global supply chain orders are delivered on time.\*\*



This dashboard identifies root causes of delivery failures across 23 global

regions and recommends targeted interventions — the kind of analysis that

saves companies millions in logistics costs annually.



\*\*Key questions answered:\*\*

\- Which regions have the worst late delivery rates?

\- Which shipping modes are underperforming?

\- Which product categories drive the most revenue?

\- Where should inventory investment be focused?



\---



\## 🔍 Key Findings



\- \*\*57.3% of orders are delivered late\*\* — massive operational gap

\- \*\*Central Africa\*\* has the worst late rate at \*\*60.7%\*\*

\- \*\*Canada\*\* performs best at \*\*51.9%\*\* late rate

\- \*\*$36.7M total revenue\*\* analyzed across global markets

\- \*\*12.1% average profit margin\*\* across all product categories

\- \*\*Class A products\*\* (top 80% revenue) need priority stock protection

## 📋 DMAIC Summary

| Phase | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **Define** | 57.3% late delivery rate — goal: improve to 70%+ on time |
| **Measure** | 103,400 late orders out of 180,519 total |
| **Analyze** | Central Africa worst (60.7%) — First Class shipping highest late rate |
| **Improve** | Carrier SLA enforcement + regional logistics partner review |
| **Control** | This live dashboard for ongoing KPI monitoring |
